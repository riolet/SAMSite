SAM renders an interactive map of a network.

It uses information from the connection logs of routers.

Using SAM, you can visualize your network as a graph of endpoints, connections, and ports, and see detailed information about the network.

It's being designed with a system architect's needs in mind to consolidate information about the health and connectivity of a network.
