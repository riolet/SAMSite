SAM is an interactive network-traffic mapping tool.

It uses information from the connection logs of routers and draws a map of the connections between hosts.

Using SAM, you can visualize your network as a graph of endpoints, connections, and ports, and see detailed information about the connections to and from each node. SAM provides a visual representation of what nodes are connecting to what other nodes on your network, and enables a high-level view of the architecture.

Designed with a system architect's needs in mind, SAM consolidates information about the health and connectivity of a network and makes it simple to comprehend.

For more information about SAM, read the about page. For a hands-on experience, try the demo!
