SAM is an interactive network-traffic mapping tool.

It uses information from the connection logs of routers and draws a map of the connections between hosts.

SAM helps you visualize of your network as a graph of endpoints, connections, and ports to provide a high level view of your network. Designed with a system architect's needs in mind, SAM consolidates information about the health and connectivity of a network and makes it simple to comprehend.

Augmenting the map, is a spreadsheet view of the network and a metadata view for a specific node, displaying info about the nodes and giving you the concrete numbers you need to make informed decisions.

For more information about SAM, read the about page. For a hands-on experience, try the demo!
